Mobile Minigames:

Mobile Minigames is a top grossing mobile game with more than 3 million downloads.
I am responsible for the client logic of several main features of the game, some of which can be seen below.

Achievements

The Achievement menu presents a list of achievements that can be unlocked by the player while playing the game. Completing achievements grants rewards in the form of "chests" and permanent buffs. This menu also lets players manage their strongest pets.


browsing the achievement menu and opening some chests

Auction House

Collectibles and NFTs can be traded in the Auction House, the center of the Mobile Minigames economy. It is possible to create buy orders and auctions, see price developments etc.


creating a pet auction and buying a pet nft

Palace

The Palace feature allows players to watch videos and streams to earn item drops, play the slot machine, claim chests and giveaways.


claiming chests, spinning the slot machine while getting stream drops
(the in-game video player is disabled in this recording)

Replay Book

The Replay feature records gameplay in the background and generates replays of special events, such as lucky slot spins. These replays can be viewed and interacted with in the Replay book.

Seasonal Challenge

The Seasonal Challenge is a monthly event that requires players to collect the ingame currency "Legendary Stars".
For every 50 stars collected, players are able to claim rewards.


claiming completed milestone rewards, buying the "Golden Battle Pass" in app purchase to get locked rewards

Streamer Talents

The Streamer Talents menu lets a player customize the talents of their heroes and improve them in combat.
